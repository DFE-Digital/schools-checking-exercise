Process description

1. Have they been diagnosed with a terminal illness?

2. Have they been diagnosed with a critical illness for more than 12 months?

3. Have they recently been diagnosed with a life changing illness?

4. Have they recently been diagnosed with a life changing injury?

  • If yes, or the answer to question 3 was yes, proceed to step 5.
  • If no, and the answer to question 3 was no, proceed to step 6.
