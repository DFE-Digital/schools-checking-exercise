Merge pupils

Design changes

Change: 'Which pupil record do you want to merge?' pupil selection is now an accessible autocomplete - Accessible autocomplete - Alpha Gov (github.com).

Reason: Some schools will have a lot of pupils, this gives them a way to find pupils through their name or UPN while still maintaining accessibility.

Severity: High

Change: On the evidence page the text area is mandatory and asks for an explanation why the merge needs to happen. This has been implemented as it's own question in the journey.

Reason: This text area is not relevant to evidence being uploaded, especially since evidence is optional and this is mandatory. This makes it more straightforward for the user to understand what is needed from them.

Severity: High

Change: Evidence screen has been removed.

Reason: The guidance says that evidence for this journey is optional.

Severity: Medium

Recommendations

Applies to: 'Explain why the pupil record needs to be merged' page

Recommendation: Include an example or 2 as hint text under the question.

Reason to change: It is not clear what we expect from users here.
