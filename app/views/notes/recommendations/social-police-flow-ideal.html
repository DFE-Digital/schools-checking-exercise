Process description

1. Have they been recently involved with social services?

  • If yes, proceed to step 3.
  • If no, proceed to step 2.

2. Have they recently been involved with the police?

  • If yes, proceed to step 3.
  • If no, proceed to step 4.

3. Has this had a severe and profound effect on their ability to access education or sit exams?

  • If yes, proceed to step 5.
  • If no, proceed to step 4.

4. Have they been detained in prison, a remand centre or secure unit for 4 months or more?

5. Have they sat any year 11 exams?

6. Summary

7. Evidence
