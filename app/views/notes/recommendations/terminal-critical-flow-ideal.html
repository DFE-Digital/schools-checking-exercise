Terminal or critical illness

Ideal flow

Open this flow chart as it's own image (opens in a new tab).

Process description

1. Have they been diagnosed with a terminal illness?

  • If yes, proceed to step 8.
  • If no, proceed to step 2.

2. Have they been diagnosed with a critical illness for more than 12 months?

  • If yes, proceed to step 3.
  • If no, proceed to step 4.

3. Has this had a severe and profound effect on their ability to access education or sit exams?

  • If yes, proceed to step 8.
  • If no, proceed to step 4.

4. Have they recently been diagnosed with a life changing illness?

  • If yes, proceed to step 5.
  • If no, proceed to step 6.

5. Has this had a severe and profound effect on their ability to access education or sit exams?

  • If yes, proceed to step 8.
  • If no, proceed to step 6.

6. Have they recently been diagnosed with a life changing injury?

  • If yes, proceed to step 7.
  • If no, proceed to step 8.

7. Has this had a severe and profound effect on their ability to access education or sit exams?

  • If yes, proceed to step 8.
  • If no, proceed to step 9.

8. Have they sat any year 11 exams?

9. Summary

10. Evidence
