Check your performance measures data (Key stage 4)

Use this service to ensure your key stage 4 (KS4) performance data is accurate before it is published in October 2024.

You can use this service to:

  • check the roll data we hold for your school or college
  • request to remove a pupil from performance calculations
  • request to merge duplicate pupil records (if you are an independent school or further education college with 14 to 16 provision)
You must read the accompanying guidance before you submit a request to remove a pupil. If your request does not meet the removal criteria, it will not be accepted.

If you do not request a change, we will use the KS4 roll data we hold for your school or college to assess secondary school performance at KS4.

Deadline for requesting a change

Submit all requests by 5pm on Friday 28 June 2024.

Who can use this service

All schools and colleges in England with KS4 provision should use this service.

This includes:

  • state funded schools
  • special schools
  • registered independent schools
  • further education colleges with 14 to 16 provision

Before you start

You will need your DfE Sign-in credentials to access this service. Create a DfE Sign-in account if you do not already have one.

After you have requested a change

We will consider your request along with the information and evidence you provide.

If we accept your request, it will show in your performance measures data when it is published in October 2024.

Get help

If you need help to submit a request or think that your pupil list is incorrect, you can:

  • send an email to CYPMD.HELPLINE@education.gov.uk
  • phone the helpdesk on 03001312762
