School Checking Exercise

This service is for making submissions to the Department for Education (DfE). Use this service to submit your School Checking data.

This includes:

  • reviewing the DfE roll data for your establishment
  • adding students to your roll
  • requesting removal of students from your roll
  • uploading files in support of your request
  • submitting all your requests to the DfE

Before you start

You need to have an account with Department for Education Sign-in to use this service.
