Check your performance measures data (Key stage 4)

Use this service to ensure your school or college performance data is accurate. This is the data we use to assess performance at key stage 4.

You can use this service to:

  • get a list of DfE roll data for your school or college
  • ask to remove a pupil from performance calculations
  • ask to merge two pupil records (if you are an independent schools or FE college with 14 to 16 provision)
All requests must be submitted by Friday 28 June 2024 at 5pm.

Before you start

You will need your DfE Sign-in credentials to access this service. Create a DfE Sign-in account if you do not already have one.

What you will need

To ask to remove a pupil, you will need to:

  • find the correct removal category
  • check what evidence you need to submit
  • ask to merge two pupil records (if you are an independent schools or FE college with 14 to 16 provision)

You can find this information in the June 2024 School checking exercise guidance.

If you do not provide the correct evidence, your request will not be accepted.

When we publish performance data

Your school or college performance data will be published in October 2024.

When you request a change using this service, we will consider it along with the evidence you provide. If we approve your request, it will be published in early 2025.
