Your pupil details

We use this list of key stage 4 pupils to calculate your school or college performance data, unless you request a change.

Where your pupil details come from

For state funded schools, key stage 4 pupils are identified at the time of the 2024 spring (January) school census, if:

  • they were on roll at the school, and
  • in year 11

If you are an independent school or further education college, your list is based on exam entries data. We explain how we collect this data in the guidance.
